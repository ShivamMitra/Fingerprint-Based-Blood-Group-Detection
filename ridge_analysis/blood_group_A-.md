# Blood Group A- Ridge Pattern Analysis

## Pattern Characteristics
- **Type**: Loops with medium ridge density
- **Typical Features**:
  - Moderate loop patterns
  - Medium ridge density
  - Slightly variable ridge flow

## Analysis
- **Ridge Orientation**: 30-75 degrees
- **Ridge Frequency**: Medium-High
- **Ridge Density**: 60-80%

## Clinical Significance
- Good correlation with A- blood group
- Moderate confidence in prediction
- Less distinct than A+ patterns

## Comparison with Other Groups
- More consistent than B- patterns
- Higher density than O- patterns
- Less complex than AB- patterns
