# Blood Group B+ Ridge Pattern Analysis

## Pattern Characteristics
- **Type**: Whorls with medium density
- **Typical Features**:
  - Distinct whorl patterns
  - Medium ridge density
  - Circular ridge flow

## Analysis
- **Ridge Orientation**: Circular patterns
- **Ridge Frequency**: Medium
- **Ridge Density**: 50-70%

## Clinical Significance
- Strong correlation with B+ blood group
- High confidence in prediction
- Unique circular patterns

## Comparison with Other Groups
- More circular than A+ patterns
- Denser than O+ patterns
- Less complex than AB+ patterns
