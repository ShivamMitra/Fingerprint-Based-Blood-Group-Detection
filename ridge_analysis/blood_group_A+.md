# Blood Group A+ Ridge Pattern Analysis

## Pattern Characteristics
- **Type**: Loops with high ridge density
- **Typical Features**:
  - Well-defined loop patterns
  - High ridge density
  - Consistent ridge flow
  - Clear deltas and cores

## Analysis
- **Ridge Orientation**: 45-90 degrees
- **Ridge Frequency**: High
- **Ridge Density**: 70-90%

## Clinical Significance
- Strong correlation with A+ blood group
- High confidence in prediction
- Distinctive pattern characteristics

## Comparison with Other Groups
- Higher ridge density than O groups
- More consistent flow than B groups
- Clearer patterns than AB groups
