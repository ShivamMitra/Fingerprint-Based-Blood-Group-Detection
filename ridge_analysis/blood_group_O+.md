# Blood Group O+ Ridge Pattern Analysis

## Pattern Characteristics
- **Type**: Arches with low density
- **Typical Features**:
  - Simple arch patterns
  - Low ridge density
  - Consistent ridge flow

## Analysis
- **Ridge Orientation**: 0-45 degrees
- **Ridge Frequency**: Low
- **Ridge Density**: 30-50%

## Clinical Significance
- Strong correlation with O+ blood group
- High confidence in prediction
- Simple, consistent patterns

## Comparison with Other Groups
- Less dense than A+ patterns
- Simpler than B+ patterns
- More consistent than AB+ patterns
